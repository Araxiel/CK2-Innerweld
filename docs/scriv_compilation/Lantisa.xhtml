Thaspitianism


A off-shot of Priarism, believing in the importance of Priaron’s wife Thaspitia and the continuation of the bloodline. Further, a very strict believe in gender roles, but rooted in “different but equal”. Women and men have strict jobs and roles, with any form of physical labour being strictly given to men, while women take care of all intellectual tasks. The head of the religion is also the head of the relative kingdom.

Before 0 is RC (Rerum caelestes), anything after 0 is SC (Sine Caelestes)1.



1 In the Realm of Gods, latinised into Rerum Deos or Rerum Caelestes. Thus the abbreviation RC for anything before 0. Subsequently, anything after 0 is Without Gods, or Sine Caelestes and thus SC.


Prehistory

Most species in the world arrived from space sometimes around 4000 RC. While the first generation tried to get off this world, soon the crashed ships were all stripped and salvaged. The first 1000 years, the colonies were keenly aware of their origins, but as more and more things started to shut down and nuclear fuel started to run out, knowledge became harder to get a hold off and what was once just common knowledge one could easily looked up, started to turn into wisdom and eventually mystical forgotten tales.

Aldterrans have become to most prevailing species in Lantisa. While little is known about the other continents, there is much evidence to suggest that Aldterrans have established themselves all across the world. Roughly one third of Aldterrans sport longer and more pointy ears, the cause of this not known, but it seems to make little to no difference.



Aldterrans are humans.

Humans Are Average



Somewhat taller than Aldterrans, they come with horns, tails and from a pale to blue to often purple complexion. They’re very alike to Aldterrans, and work among and with them about as often as the two species butt heads.

Middleweld is the place of much internal strive, but culturally very connected. Isenreik will claim ownership over it.


Costanorda is a wealthy and powerful region with a great and comfortable climate. Very similar to Italy.

Merkkantia is a small island nation off the coast. The people are called the Merkkantini. While in early times, they were mainly pirates and raiders, they started to rob more than they needed from Costanorda and sold it to the people of Middleweld. With enough time, they started to transition and transform their raiding ships into ships of trade.



Insaliun is a island archipelago split between many rivalling factions. Powerful once united in the Blue Union


A rather cold and forested southern region. Often at war with Middleweld, Delvadekia is often a thorn in the eye of Isenreik and other Middleweld kingdoms. Especially the south-eastern part is considered very backwards.



The Vortisi are a hardy and paranoid people. Often having been the target of foreign raids and invasions, they have fortified themselves in great castles and are keeping a good eye on any foreigners and forcing them to pay a hefty toll.


Hisoguk is a cold and forgiving desolate land.


Rejsuk is a freezing coastal area, but it has plenty of resources. The Rejsuk people have little similarities with the other people of Lantisa, and many, including themselves, believe they are actually the descendants of some colonizers from across the ocean.


Yasilan is a prosperous little region wedged in between the inner sea and the ocean.


Estapia is a land of both great steppes and fertile coast.
